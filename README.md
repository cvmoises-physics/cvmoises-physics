### 🚀 About Me

I am a Junior Applied Physics undergrad from the Philippines. I am a student from the University of the Philippines, Los Baños currently doing research on two-dimensional gauss-hermite states of the quantum free particle and looking at certain formal mappings with the Schrodinger equation and the Paraxial Wave equation in classical optics. In the future, I am looking forward in doing theoretical physics, also maybe some in quantum information, some with plasmas, fusion research, and high energy physics.

Doing physics was actually just a pastime to the point that things get intriguing and so absurd that I started to keep chasing the pleasure of understanding them. It is mostly with the awe on the limits of human's capability of articulating natural phenomena and that the precision of these articulations allowed us to construct human civilization of today. Getting into physics was pure passion, with humility to its ability in giving us profound understanding of the universe and disover its secrets.
